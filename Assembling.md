# Assembling Oblivion
Oblivion's chassis and lid has been made with TPU completely. The material is flexible and can withstand very hard blows. However the mounts insidde the chassis are not very sturdy therefore components may find a way to squeeze out.

Therefore in the future, design base including mounts with sturdy plastic, design walls with TPU.
