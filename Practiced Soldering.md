# Always keep in mind
- Keep the tip of the soldering iron shiny always clean it

Using solid rosin flux and a sponge to clean the tip of my solder cut short options to solder smaller components 

Practice soldering on two wires
- Recommended to twist both wires before soldering
- apply flux to ensure wire sticks in place
- Use a clean tip, apply flux and tin the tip so that it stops from oxidizing
- use solder wire on the tip to make a ball and then slightly rub it across the connection of the wire putting solder on in the process

## Why one needs to learn how to Solder
- To solder together wires in this case to join battery input of two esc's into one
- To solder wires to motor
- To solder two safety switches into one and attach a safety light diode to ensure bot is armed and ready to fight
